Part of the ongoing album notes in [`You Look Pretty Sad For A Girl So In Love — Raw Thoughts`]({% post_url 2026-06-15-YLPSFAGSIL-RAW-THROUGHTS %}).

This song most directly highlights the central conflict in Olivia's relationship. There is another woman who is getting in the way. The song does not suggest infidelity, but more like this person is crossing a boundary that Olivia is not comfortable with. In addition, as we will discover later, her man does not agree with the boundary that Olivia is setting and is not on her side.

I don't think there is anything in the lyrics I need to call out, the song itself is direct and she says what she means and means what she says. Just in the narrative, this is the most overt explanation of what is going wrong in the relationship.
