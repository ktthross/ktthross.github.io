Part of the ongoing album notes in [`You Look Pretty Sad For A Girl So In Love — Raw Thoughts`]({% post_url 2026-06-15-YLPSFAGSIL-RAW-THROUGHTS %}).

Now we come to the second single off the album. A song that really stands out as it is the turning point for the narrative, and signals the transition from the happy but sad songs that indicate trouble ahead to the sad but sad songs that deal with the fallout and reckoning of the second half.

The song it particularly important since it is the source of the name of the Unraveled Tour.

> All the pretty girls in the foreground of my mind
> I thought I'd done enough, but they keep moving the line
> I thought I found the antidote this time
> I thought I found the antidote this time

Comparing herself to other girls, admiring other girls and feeling inadequate compared to others is a common theme in Olivia's music. She is, and thought she found, an antidote to this comparison.
