The next song and the immediate stand out to me on the record `U + Me = <3`.

There are only a couple things to say about this. 

Its so god damn catchy and fun its gotta be one of my favorites.
The lyrics capture the feeling of being in love so well.

Bridge:

> Sometimes, I get overwhelmed and way too far ahead of myself
> I often get the feeling that I'll never want somebody else and

We see this same theme of being out of control or manic.  Of seeing things in absolute terms.

Chorus:
    
> 'Cause I got a feeling wounds are healing, talking on the phone

There is a reference to some sort of conflict between them. They are reconciling from some sort of argument and Olivia is feeling optimistic about the relationship. Its a subtle clue dropped into this track which is 🤌😚.

Again there is this distance: they are talking on the phone, they are not together. So far there is only one song on this album where the two are physically together, on Honeybee, and that song is low key sort of sad.


Sounds good!
